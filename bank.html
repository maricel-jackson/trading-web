Major Bank in Malaysia

Maybank (Malayan Banking Berhad)

  • In terms of deposits, assets, and pretty much every other indicator, Maybank is the top and biggest bank in Malaysia.

  • Maybank’s adaptability and dedication to a wide range of customers are two of its most important advantages. Maybank provides almost every kind of service and can accommodate any size business with its extensive array of personal and business banking options.

  • Maybank has a large number of overseas branches throughout Southeast Asia, ranging from China to Cambodia. Maybank, one of the biggest banks in ASEAN, is a genuinely global organization.

  • Through its community initiatives and Islamic banking methods, Maybank supports Malaysia’s economic expansion and development in addition to its main banking services.
