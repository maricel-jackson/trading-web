Market Entry Strategies

Possible Market Entry Strategies to Promote Products or Services in Malaysia, I can consider various Market Entry Strategies based on the target audience, business goals, and the competitive landscape. Here are some of the strategies that will likely do:

  • Direct Exporting:


    - Selling directly to international customers or distributors.
    - Offers higher profit margins but requires managing shipping and logistics.

  • Partnerships or Joint Ventures:


    - Collaborating with local distributors or businesses in foreign markets.
    - Helps reduce risks and share market expertise.

  • Franchising:


    - Licensing the Jackmar Trading brand to local entrepreneurs.
    - Allows for rapid market expansion with less financial risk.

  • E-commerce & Digital Platforms:


    - Leveraging online trade platforms for B2B and B2C sales.
    - Reduces overhead costs and enables global reach.

  • Strategic Alliances:


    - Partnering with logistics or shipping companies to streamline operations.
    - Enhances efficiency and lowers transportation costs.
