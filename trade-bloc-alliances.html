Welcome to Global Trade Bloc Alliances

Malaysia actively participates in several trade bloc alliances, each aiming to enhance economic cooperation and integration among member countries.

ASEAN

Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN) Promotes economic growth and regional stability among its 10 member states.​

CPTPP

CPTPP (Comprehensive and Progressive Agreement for Trans-Pacific Partnership) – Boosts trade by reducing trade barriers and improving market access.

RCEP

RCEP (Regional Comprehensive Economic Partnership) – A free trade agreement between ASEAN and five other nations, aiming to lower tariffs and improve trade relations. ​

APEC

APEC (Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation) – Enhances regional economic integration and cooperation among Asia-Pacific economies.​

WTO

WTO (World Trade Organization) – Ensures global trade rules and resolves trade disputes.​
